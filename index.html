How it works

⏰  Set your alarm like any other
📺  When it rings, watch a short ad
❤️  Ad revenue becomes Charity Points
🌍  Points fund causes that matter

Why Pinaki exists

Small actions, repeated every day by many people, add up to something enormous. Pinaki turns the few seconds after your alarm rings — seconds you were going to spend anyway — into funding for meaningful causes.

It costs you nothing. No donations, no volunteering. Just wake up — and choose good.
