Why Pinaki exists

In May 2026, I became a father.

Looking at my son in those first days, I kept circling one thought: what kind of world is he going to grow up in — and what am I doing about it?

I knew that small actions, repeated every day by many people, can add up to something enormous. The problem is that most of us don't have a way to contribute daily that doesn't cost money we can't spare or time we don't have.

So I built one.

The app carries my son's name — a name that speaks of one who wields a divine instrument. I like to think of it as a tool. A tool in the hands of someone trying to do good.

That's what Pinaki Alarm is. A tool. In your hands.

What exists today

  • ✅ Live on Google Play — launching publicly August 1st

What your support builds

🎯 Reaching people — an app like this lives or dies by community size. Marketing brings Pinaki to the millions of mornings it needs to matter.

🤝 Real partnerships — formal relationships with verified charities and NGOs, so every Charity Point has a transparent, documented destination.

🔬 Transparency systems — public impact reports and cause voting, so you always know where the money goes.

🏫 The bigger vision: free education hubs — spaces offering completely free education to children who can't afford it, funded by nothing more than the world waking up each morning.
