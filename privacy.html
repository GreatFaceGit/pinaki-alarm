Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Pinaki Alarm ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account and use Pinaki Alarm, we collect:

  • Email address — used to create and manage your account
  • Alarm times — the alarms you set within the app
  • Usage data — number of ads watched, ads skipped, and charity points earned
  • Ad interaction data — timestamps of when you watched or skipped an ad

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Pinaki Alarm service
  • To save your alarms and charity points across devices and sessions
  • To track ad engagement for the purpose of generating charitable revenue
  • To improve the app based on usage patterns

3. Advertising

Pinaki Alarm uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show you personalised ads based on your browsing activity. You will be shown a consent prompt before any personalised advertising is enabled.

For more information on how Google uses your data, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at adssettings.google.com.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. Data is stored on servers within Supabase's infrastructure. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties other than those required to operate the service (Supabase, Google AdSense).

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for personalised advertising at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

7. Cookies

Pinaki Alarm itself does not use cookies. However, Google AdSense may set cookies on your device for the purpose of serving and measuring advertisements. You can manage cookie preferences through the consent prompt shown in the app.

8. Children's Privacy

Pinaki Alarm is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
